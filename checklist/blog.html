Hello, this blog post is going to be kind of different, in that it is more of an amalgamation of resources I keep up with as kind of a checklist for web security. I've always been pretty interested in computer security in general, and my emphasis throughout college was in web development, so web security is my happy ground between the two topics. These resources and tips are things I keep in mind while making web apps and working on projects, or even maintaining my website in general.
